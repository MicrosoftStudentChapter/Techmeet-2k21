About Event

The techmeet is one of the Microsoft Learn Student Chapter's (MLSC) most anticipated events anually. We are pumped this year to finally conduct the event offline again. The meet starts with the introduction of MLSC and the Core Team. MLSC Team members will then give short presentations on the various fields. This year in addition to Machine Learning, Web Development, Competitive Programming, by popular demand we'll be conducting workshops on Game Development and Ethical Hacking as well.
MLSC has always encouraged interactions in the open source community, and looks forward to holding Q&A sessions in the Techmeet. Process for the recruitments for the new term will be shared in the meet as well. We as a team, are determined to bring out the best of the tech community around us, and we're sure that Techmeet will be a worthwhile event for all those looking for a sense of direction in order to pursue their passion in various technical fields. Don't forget to mark the date!!




About MLSC

Microsoft Learn Student Chapter is a specialized society which is devoted to elevate the coding culture of Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology by mentoring and motivating its scholars to upgrade their critical thinking, logical reasoning and coding skills. One of the elite societies of TIET. MLSC was formed with the objective of furnishing scholars a dynamic platform which feeds their curiousity and develops their specialized skills. MLSC aims to produce a community of versatile enthusiasts with progressive mindset, promoting development holistically which includes injecting soft skills as well. MLSC effectuates it by organizing multiple technology driven events including tech meetups and Hackathons and promoting team structure. In the long run, MLSC plans to cultivate and nurture youthful minds of TIET to a magnitude where they're complete enough to contend amongst the classic, therefore upheaving the name of TIET.
